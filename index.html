Work Expreince

I have been doing photography for the last 3 years. I started my career as Cinematographer 2 years ago. I shot my first action film - 'Escape'. as a writer, cinematographer, director, and editor, 2 years ago. Each and every part of the film was done by me (one-man crew) except acting. I have even performed as an action director as every action and moves were directed by me.

Last year in October 2017, I shot my first commercial video advertisement as a cinematographer for Kidzee Playgroup School. Just after that, I shot commercial for Prerna Creation and documentary for Nehru Gram Bharti University, Allahabad. In the month of November 2017, shot two commercial advertisement video for Umang Sarees and Scanner Classes as an Assistant Cinematographer and Lead Photographer.

As a photographer, I have been a Wedding photographer and commercial photographer as well. I have shot photos for one of the finest brands in Allahabad city, like Paradise Bakers, Madan Men's Showroom, Hotel Kanha Residency, etc.
